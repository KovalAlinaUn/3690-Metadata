Steady Warriors is a defense survival game with roguelike and gacha elements. Your task is to assemble a squad of heroes that will defend the farm through four seasons of chaos. 

Start the game with available heroes, defend through ten waves, each wave have assortment of different enemies and some waves are elite with final wave being boss wave to pass the level. Each season has three levels with progressive difficulty. When you start a level pick two character to begin with, then each wave you will be able either to expand your line of heroes or to level one of them. Defend farm, beat monsters, earn coins. 

Spend your earned coin on obtaining new heroes or upgradding ones you have in your pack. Every character have assortment of stats like Health, Attack, and Attack Speed. Upgrading the stats improves the baseline which makes levelups even more powerfull. Also you can spend coins on buying bonus items like healing and bomb to deal damage to the enemies.
